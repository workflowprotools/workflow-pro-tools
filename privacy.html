Privacy Policy

Workflow Pro Tools

Last Updated: June 26, 2025

Overview

Workflow Pro Tools is committed to protecting your privacy. This policy explains how our LinkedIn Growth Assistant and WooCommerce Bulk Importer tools handle your information.

Data Collection

We do not collect, store, or transmit any personal information. All data processing happens locally on your device.

LinkedIn Growth Assistant

  • Extension only accesses public LinkedIn pages you visit
  • Your LinkedIn credentials are never accessed or stored
  • Connection statistics stored locally in your browser
  • No external servers or databases involved
  • All automation happens within your browser

WooCommerce Bulk Importer

  • Plugin processes CSV files locally on your WordPress site
  • No product data transmitted to external servers
  • Import history stored in your WordPress database
  • All file processing happens on your hosting server
  • No external API calls or data sharing

Data Storage

  • Chrome Extension: Uses Chrome's local storage API
  • WordPress Plugin: Uses your WordPress database
  • No cloud storage or external data transmission
  • You maintain complete control over your data
  • Data can be deleted by uninstalling the tools

Third-Party Services

  • LinkedIn Extension: Works with publicly visible LinkedIn profiles
  • WooCommerce Plugin: Integrates with WooCommerce's existing data structure
  • Both tools respect the terms of service of their respective platforms
  • No unauthorized access to private information

Security

  • All tools include built-in safety measures
  • Rate limiting prevents account issues
  • No sensitive data transmission
  • Local processing ensures data security

Updates to This Policy

This privacy policy may be updated periodically to reflect changes in our tools or legal requirements. Continued use of our tools constitutes acceptance of any changes.
